Our Story

Born in trap.
Built for everything.

stxrm808 started with one problem: the most interesting producers in the world were working with tools that weren't built for them. Presets designed for everyone. Libraries that sounded like the same album, over and over.

We built stxrm808 because the best sound design is deeply personal. The 808 that's yours shouldn't sound like the one everyone else downloaded. The atmosphere on your record should have your fingerprint on it — not a preset number.

We started in trap and hip hop because that's where precision matters most. Where a quarter-note slide can define a record. Where the difference between a flat kick and one that hits is felt in the chest, not just heard.

But the DNA never stops there. Sound evolves. Producers evolve. The tools we build are built to evolve with you — from trap to cinematic, from 808s to orchestral, from beat making to full production. Your sound is your stxrm808. We just give you the tools to express it.

What We Stand For

The principles behind every plugin.

01

Sound quality is non-negotiable.

We don't ship plugins until they sound better than anything else at their price point. That's the bar. Every time.

02

Tools should disappear.

The best plugin is the one you forget you're using. Interfaces that get out of your way. Workflows that feel natural. Less menu-diving, more music.

03

Culture first, always.

Trap and hip hop didn't become the dominant musical force of the last two decades by accident. We respect where we came from and build for where it's going.
